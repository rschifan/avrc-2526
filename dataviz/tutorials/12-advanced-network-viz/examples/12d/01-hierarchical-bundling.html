12d — Hierarchical edge bundling

The full GoT network as a bundled radial diagram. Characters sit on the rim grouped by community; edges curve toward the centre, bundled through a synthetic root → community → character hierarchy. Source tutorial →

What this example shows

  • A hierarchy makes the bundles. The flat node list is wrapped in a two-level tree; d3.cluster places leaves on a circle, and d3.lineRadial + curveBundle.beta(0.85) routes each edge through its least-common ancestor.
  • Hover a leaf — its edges take the colour of the community they connect to, so you can see at a glance which groups a character reaches; every other edge fades.
  • Community bands on the rim make the group structure instantly legible.

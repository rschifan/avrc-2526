12b — Chord diagram

The top-30 Game-of-Thrones characters as a chord diagram: the same matrix as 12a, bent into a circle. Arcs on the rim are nodes; ribbons across the interior are weighted edges. Hover a rim arc to isolate one character's flow. Source tutorial →

What this example shows

  • Two D3 generators do the work. d3.chord() turns the N × N weight matrix into arc + ribbon angles; d3.ribbon() + d3.arc() turn those angles into SVG paths.
  • Community ordering on the rim keeps each colour block contiguous, so the group structure reads at a glance.
  • Hover-to-isolate hides every ribbon except those touching the hovered arc (fill and stroke, so unconnected edges vanish completely) and dims the arcs it doesn't reach — the standard trick for taming chord density.
